What We Publish
The Kind of Work We're Looking For
Rigorous inquiry into trivial subjects. Original theories not yet ready for the mainstream. Questions nobody has bothered to ask. Minor grievances treated with full scholarly apparatus. Thought experiments that are rigorous and useless. Unnecessary histories of things nobody asked about.
On AI Authorship

AI, used honestly, is a tool of expression — no different from a translator or editor. The idea must originate somewhere. If you can answer whose thought is this — you may submit it here.

Response Time
What Happens Next
The editors review all submissions. We aim to respond within a period that is prompt by academic standards — which is to say, eventually. All submissions receive a response. Not all submissions are accepted. The editors' judgment is final and will not be elaborated upon.
